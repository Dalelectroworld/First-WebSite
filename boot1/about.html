About Us

Our Business Approach Our business approach requires an on-site project team of experienced professionals and skilled workers dedicated to your projects/facilities. This approach is structured to effectively support our entire contract portfolio regardless of location. Our on-site service delivery model gives us the strength and to respond to client needs at each contract location, where each team benefits from tapping our corporate resources for complete and unparalleled contract support in operational leadership continuing business relations, supply chain management, and human resources.

VISION STATEMENT

To be a highly successful in all electrical, projects and facility management, maintenance and construction works in the global market and to become the most preferred choice by attaining excellence in quality. DALELECTROWORLD will provide a wide range of services in the field of power generation, transmission and distribution, and electricity market.

CORE VALUES

1. Innovation; We are passionate about creating new solutions for crafting a better tomorrow for our communities and country at large.
2. Dependability; We are reliable and trustworthy at any exchange.
3. Integrity; We will always safeguard the integrity of our employees, customers or company.
4. Quality; We will not compromise materials to be used.
5. Customer service; We are committed to total satisfaction to meet or exceed customer expectations.
